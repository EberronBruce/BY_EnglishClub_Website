Who We Are

We are a couple living in Ho Chi Minh City near the Saigon Bridge, who opened up an English club at our house. The native English speaking instructor is from America. He has a TESOL Certification with Business English Specialization. He has lectured at University of Economics and Finance in Ho Chi Minh City. The instructor also has experience in teaching English in Korea and Vietnam.

The co-teacher is a Vietnamese native who is well adverse in the English language. She studied graduate courses in Korea and now currently works on the Metro project for the development of the Subway system in Ho Chi Minh City. She is bilingual in both Vietnamese and English. She helps with explanation when you get stuck with English.

About the Club

The English club is a casual, friendly, and comfortable environment for adults to practice and study English. The topics of the class will vary from daily life, business, and occasionally special topics to keep the class interesting. This will help broaden your English vocabulary and knowledge base which will make you feel more comfortable in any environment. The instructors will aid you in your confidence in using English, as well as, correcting your pronunciation and increasing your vocabulary. For those of you who that need extra assistance, a Vietnamese co-instructor will be there to aid you in the class. Occasionally, upon student demand, picnics, outdoor BBQs, and small house parties will be created to provide a real English environment for students. The class will not exceed more than 10 students.
